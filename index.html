About Amanda

Professional Beach Volleyball Player

Amanda Harnett is a Canadian professional beach volleyball player competing on the international stage. Based in Los Angeles, she competes in the AVP Pro Series, FIVB World Tour, and NORCECA competitions.

With her sights set on the 2028 and 2032 Olympic Games, Amanda continues to refine her craft and push the boundaries of her sport. Her unique background—from Canadian roots to California training—brings a distinctive perspective to beach volleyball.

A Message from Amanda

My journey from the frozen Canadian landscape to the golden California beaches taught me something powerful: our environment doesn't define us—our mindset does. Every day I step onto the sand, I carry the resilience I built in the snow. This perspective drives everything I do.

Beach volleyball is my arena, but my mission extends far beyond the court. I believe in empowering the next generation—especially young women—to build confidence, embrace their inner strength, and chase dreams that might seem impossible. That's why I created Piggy & Kay, a children's book series focused on building confidence and positive inner psychology from an early age.

Whether I'm competing on the AVP Pro Tour, working with partners who share my values, or coaching athletes to unlock their potential, I'm guided by one principle: lift as you climb. I want to show that excellence in sport and meaningful impact in communities aren't separate paths—they're the same journey.
