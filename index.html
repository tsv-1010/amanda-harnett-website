My journey from the frozen Canadian landscape to the golden California beaches taught me something powerful: our environment doesn't define us‚Äîour mindset does. Every day I step onto the sand, I carry the resilience I built in the snow. This perspective drives everything I do.

Beach volleyball is my arena, but my mission extends far beyond the court. I believe in empowering the next generation‚Äîespecially young women‚Äîto build confidence, embrace their inner strength, and chase dreams that might seem impossible.
